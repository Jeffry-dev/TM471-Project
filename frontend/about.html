About Cedars of Lebanon

A family-run Lebanese restaurant bringing authentic Mediterranean flavors to your table with traditional recipes, charcoal grills, and warm hospitality that makes every meal feel like home.

Our chefs use traditional Lebanese cooking methods and source the finest Mediterranean ingredients, spices, and premium halal meats.

The menu features classic mezze, charcoal-grilled kebabs, fresh manakish, and authentic desserts made with love using family recipes.

Whether you're joining us for a casual meal, family gathering, or a special celebration, we want Cedars of Lebanon to feel like your second home.
